Privacy Policy

How Iron Lantern Studios™ collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

Iron Lantern Studios™ (“we,” “us,” or “our”) operates BrickedMyPortfolio.com (the “Site”). This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, and any details you provide via contact forms or newsletter sign–ups.
  • Usage Data: Pages viewed, time spent, device and browser information, collected automatically via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Log Data: Server logs including IP address, access timestamps, and error reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To send you newsletters or updates if you opt in.
  • To analyze and improve our Site’s performance and usability.
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security risks.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies to customize your experience and gather usage data. You can disable cookies via your browser settings, but some Site features may not function properly without them.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service Providers: Third–party vendors assisting with hosting, analytics, or email delivery.
  • Legal Compliance: Law enforcement or government authorities when required by law.

5. Third–Party Links

Our Site may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Review their policies before sharing personal information.

6. Children’s Privacy

We do not target or knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us to request deletion.

7. Your Rights & Choices

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your data, subject to legal obligations.
  • Opt–Out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications via the link in emails.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Revisions will be posted here with a “Last Updated” date. Your continued use of the Site constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

For questions or concerns about this policy, contact us at Jason.harvey@brickedmyportfolio.com .
