About Jason Harvey

I’m Jason Harvey, the solo indie developer behind Iron Lantern Studios™ LLC. I blend creative artistry and technical innovation to build stylized worlds and intuitive gameplay systems.

My core expertise spans AI pathfinding, custom shaders, and modular environment kits. Whether prototyping an FPS or sculpting asset packs, I approach every challenge with curiosity and precision.

This site showcases my current projects, prototypes, and asset packs available for purchase. Feel free to explore my work or reach out to collaborate on your next idea.
