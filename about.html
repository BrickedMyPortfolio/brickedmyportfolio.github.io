About Jason & Iron Lantern Studios™

Who I Am

Hey, I’m Jason. I started Iron Lantern Studios™ to create the kinds of games I always wanted to play—fast-paced, low-spec, innovative, and built with intent.

I work solo or with micro teams to build functional prototypes, asset kits, shaders, and eventually publishable titles. Whether it’s UI design or engine scripting, I’m hands-on with every aspect.

Studio History

  • 2022: Founded Iron Lantern Studios™.
  • 2023: Released first asset pack & internal prototype.
  • 2024: Game jam finalist and shader library launch.

Dev Philosophy

Design first. Optimize later. Fun is the core feature. I try not to overengineer and always aim to support lower-end hardware while exploring niche mechanics or movement tech.

"Bricked? Perfect. Iterate smarter next time."
