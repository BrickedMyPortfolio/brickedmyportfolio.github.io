About Me

Hey, I'm Jason — a curious and resourceful tinkerer with a passion for game development and hardware hacking. I thrive on hands-on experimentation, whether it's reverse engineering IR signals, building smart LED control systems, or exploring unconventional setups like running Linux on microcontrollers.

My projects blend creativity with technical depth. I love repurposing components, integrating microcontrollers like Raspberry Pi Pico and Arduino, and pushing the boundaries of what's possible with DIY electronics. Whether it's crafting immersive audio experiences or designing custom control systems, I’m always chasing the next challenge.

This site is my digital playground — a space to showcase my games, share my assets, and document the journey. Thanks for stopping by!
