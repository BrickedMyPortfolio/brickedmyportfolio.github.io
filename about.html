About Me

Jason & Iron Lantern Studios™

I'm a 3D game developer and digital asset creator — I make games, models, and chaos.

This site is a home for my portfolio, my projects, and my creative wreckage.

Over the years, I've experimented with various game engines, styles, and workflows, learning more from my failures than my successes.

From pixel art mishaps to 3D model nightmares, everything you see here has a story — some broken, some brilliant, all real.

I'm passionate about pushing limits, embracing glitches, and sharing my journey, whether it's polished or perfectly flawed.

Thanks for stopping by this gloriously bricked corner of the internet.
