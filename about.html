About Iron Lantern Studios™

Crafting immersive indie game experiences with passion and precision.

The Developer

I'm Jason Harvey, the founder and solo developer behind Iron Lantern Studios™ LLC. My journey began with a passion for both art and code, which eventually fused into a career crafting unique game experiences.

Over the years, I've honed my skills in 3D environment design, shader programming, and gameplay mechanics. I love creating modular asset packs that empower other developers to bring their visions to life.

When I'm not coding or creating assets, I enjoy exploring indie game jams, learning new tools, and collaborating with fellow devs across the globe.

Mission & Philosophy

At Iron Lantern Studios™, my mission is to blend artistic flair with solid technical foundations. I believe every game should tell a story, whether through a seamless gameplay loop, a memorable world, or an intuitive interface.

Quality and polish are at the heart of my workflow. From iterative prototyping to final performance tweaks, I make sure each piece of content meets rigorous standards before release.

  • Creativity: Pushing boundaries in art and design.
  • Resilience: Overcoming technical challenges with smart solutions.
  • Collaboration: Sharing assets and knowledge to uplift the indie community.
  • Quality: Ensuring every project shines on its own merits.
